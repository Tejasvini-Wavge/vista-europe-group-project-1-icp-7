Paris, France

Paris, the capital city of France, is globally celebrated for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine, and is renowned for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum.
